OUR WORK

At Mentally Up, we offer a range of mental health services to help you improve your well-being. Our services include individual counseling, couples counseling, family therapy, group therapy, and online therapy. We also offer support groups for individuals dealing with specific issues, such as addiction, trauma, and grief. Our mental health professionals use evidence-based treatment methods to help you achieve your goals and overcome any obstacles you may be facing. We're committed to providing you with the support and guidance you need to feel mentally and emotionally empowered.

Our Skills.

At Mentally Up, our licensed mental health professionals are trained to help individuals facing a range of mental health challenges. We use evidence-based treatment methods and tailor our approach to meet your unique needs and goals. Our skills include:
Counseling and therapy: We offer individual counseling, couples counseling, and family therapy to help you work through personal challenges and improve your relationships.

Support groups: We provide safe and supportive environments for individuals dealing with addiction, trauma, and grief to connect and share their experiences.

Online therapy: We offer convenient and secure online therapy sessions for individuals who prefer the flexibility and privacy of virtual counseling.

Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT): Our therapists are trained in CBT, a proven approach that helps individuals identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors.

Mindfulness-based therapy: We offer mindfulness-based therapy, which emphasizes present-moment awareness and non-judgmental acceptance to reduce stress and improve emotional regulation.
At Mentally Up, we're committed to helping you feel mentally and emotionally empowered. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can support you.
